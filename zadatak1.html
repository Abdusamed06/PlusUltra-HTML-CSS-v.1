Tom Ellis

TV ACTOR

Birthday:

November 17, 1978

Birthplace:

Bangor, Wales

Age:

43 years old

About

Welsh actor best known for his role as Dr. Oliver Cousins on the television soap opera EastEnders.
He gained further fame starring as the titular role on FOX's Lucifer.

Before Fame

Before booking his first acting role at age 22 on the series Kiss Me Kate,
he was a French horn player for the City of Sheffield Youth Orchestra.

Trivia

He also had film roles in Buffalo Soldiers (2001) and Vera Drake (2004).

Family Life

He was married to Tamzin Outhwaite from 2006 until 2014. In June 2019,
he married Producer Meaghan Oppenheimer. He has a total of three daughters.

Associated With

He acted alongside Anna Chancellor in the short-lived series Suburban Shootout.
